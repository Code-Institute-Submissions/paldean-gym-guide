Your guide to success on defeating the eight gym leaders of Paldea

The Eight Gym Leaders of Paldea

Katy - Bug

Bug type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Flying and Rock type Pokémon.

Brassius - Grass

Grass type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Flying, Bug, Poison and Ice type Pokémon.

Iono - Electric

Electric type Pokémon are weak to Ground type Pokémon.

Kofu - Water

Water type Pokémon are weak to Grass and Electric type Pokémon.

Larry - Normal

Normal type Pokémon are weak to Fighting type Pokémon.

Ryme - Ghost

Ghost type Pokémon are weak to Ghost and Dark type Pokémon.

Tulip - Psychic

Psychic type Pokémon are weak to Bug, Ghost and Dark type Pokémon.

Grusha - Ice

Ice type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Fighting, Steel and Rock type Pokémon.
