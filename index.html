The Eight Gym Leaders of Paldea

Bug

Bug type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Flying and Rock type and resistant to Fighting, Grass and Ground type Pokémon.

Grass

Grass type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Flying, Bug, Poison and Ice type and resistant to Grass, Water and Ground type Pokémon.

Electric

Electric type Pokémon are weak to Ground type and resistant to Electric type Pokémon.

Water

Water type Pokémon are weak to Grass and Electric type and resistant to Water, Fire, Ice and Steel type Pokémon.

center

Normal

Normal type Pokémon are weak to Fighting type and immune to Ghost type Pokémon.

Ghost

Ghost type Pokémon are weak to Ghost and Dark type and immune to Normal and Fighting type Pokémon.

Psychic

Psychic type Pokémon are weak to Bug and Dark type and resistant to Psychic and Fighting type Pokémon.

Ice

Ice type Pokémon are weak to Fire, Fighting, Steel and Rock type and resistant to Ice type Pokémon.
